Deutsch — verbs & nouns

The objective: master all 1120 words — 120 verbs and 1000 nouns — so you can translate any of them on sight. Each correct drill answer marks a word as mastered.

Nouns are weighted for the humanities: Levels 1–10 are everyday high-frequency vocabulary; Levels 11–25 build the German philosophical vocabulary — being, mind, ethics, politics, aesthetics, knowledge, language, culture, religion, nature, phenomenology, critical theory, psychology, economy, theology.

Ranks track your journey from Anfänger (0) to Philosoph (all 1120).
